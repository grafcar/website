About Me

Hey there, I'm Bryan De Los Santos—a software developer who's all about bringing ideas to life through code and design.

I graduated with a Computer Science degree from Cal State Fullerton and have since been diving deep into frontend and full-stack development. Right now, I'm steering the digital strategy and e-commerce efforts at Elit Tile Corp, where I handle everything from Shopify systems to crafting smooth UI/UX and making data-driven decisions to boost business growth.

I love blending creativity with tech. Whether it's building over 200 client websites, setting up servers on a Raspberry Pi, or writing scalable code, I'm happiest when I'm solving problems in dynamic, fast-moving settings. My toolkit includes modern web tech like React, Node.js, and GraphQL, plus mobile development with React Native and Swift, and backend automation using Python, APIs, and databases.

Outside of coding, you'll find me geeking out over Arduino projects, tinkering with my home network setup, or nerding out on UI design—think color theory, spacing, and slick motion. I'm fueled by curiosity, a love for craftsmanship, and a knack for creating user experiences that just feel right.
