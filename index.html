About Me

Hey, I'm Bryan De Los Santos

I'm a software developer with a passion for turning ideas into reality through clean code and thoughtful design. With a Computer Science degree from Cal State Fullerton, I've been immersed in the world of frontend and full-stack development, bringing digital experiences to life.

Currently, I'm leading digital strategy and e-commerce at Elit Tile Corp, where I manage Shopify ecosystems, craft intuitive UI/UX, and leverage data to drive business growth. From building over 200 client websites to setting up Raspberry Pi servers or writing scalable code, I thrive in fast-paced environments where creative problem-solving is key.

My tech toolkit is packed with modern tools like React, Node.js, GraphQL, React Native, and Swift for mobile apps, plus Python, APIs, and databases for backend automation. But it's not just about the tech—I'm obsessed with the art of design. Whether it's perfecting color theory, fine-tuning spacing, or adding slick motion to interfaces, I'm all about creating user experiences that feel seamless and intuitive.

When I'm not coding, you'll catch me tinkering with Arduino projects, optimizing my home network, or geeking out over UI design. Driven by curiosity and a love for craftsmanship, I'm always chasing the next challenge to build something that's not just functional, but feels just right.
